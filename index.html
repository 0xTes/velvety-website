Questions & Answers

The things creators ask us most

Is this legal and compliant with FOSTA/SESTA? +
Yes. Every page, every word of copy, and every strategy we implement is designed with FOSTA/SESTA compliance in mind. We focus on content subscriptions, personal branding, and social engagement — never explicit transactional language. We review compliance regularly as regulations evolve.
What if my OnlyFans or Fansly account is banned? +
This is exactly why we prioritise email list building. Your email list sits outside any platform, hosted on infrastructure you control. Even if every subscription platform disappeared tomorrow, you would still have direct access to every subscriber.
How long until I see results? +
Your resource page goes live within 7–14 days of your onboarding call. Email subscribers typically start flowing within the first week. SEO results take 60–90 days to build meaningfully, but the compounding effect is worth it.
Do I need technical knowledge to work with you? +
Absolutely not. We handle everything — domain, hosting, design, email platform setup, automation, and ongoing management. If you can log into your email, you have all the tech skills needed to work with Velvety.
Will my real name or personal information be exposed? +
Never. We build everything under your creator persona, handle all domain and hosting registrations with privacy protection, and have strict NDAs in place. Your privacy is non-negotiable to us.
What platforms do you support linking to? +
We support all major creator platforms: OnlyFans, Fansly, AllAccessFans, Throne, and more. We also integrate with your social media accounts, wishlist pages, and any other links you want to include. Everything lives on your resource page.
